Integrates Gravity Forms with Email Marketing tool Drip.

== Description ==

[Drip](https://www.getdrip.com) is email marketing automation that doesn't suck. It's
the easiest way to build your email list and engage your subscribers through an email 
mini-course. Easily send emails to your prospects, trial users and customers based on their
behavior.

This plugin makes it dead simple to route your Gravity Forms form data into Drip, 
including creating subscribers, custom fields and new events in Drip.

= The Drip Process =

Simply [sign up for a Drip account](https://www.getdrip.com/pricing) and install this
plugin to get started integrating your Gravity Forms with Drip's email marketing software.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the drip-gravity-forms folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
1. Activate the Drip Email Marketing Add-On for Gravity Forms plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Configure the plugin by going to the Gravity Forms settings menu, and selecting Drip from the submenu
